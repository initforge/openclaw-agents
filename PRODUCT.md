# Product

## Register

product

## Users

OpenClaw Ops is used by an operator who manages trading automation, Facebook lead response and platform content from one laptop. The user needs speed, clear risk context and evidence for why an agent acted.

## Product Purpose

OpenClaw Ops is a three-workspace agent operations console. It lets the operator monitor state, review risk, approve gated actions, preserve customer relationships and run content workflows without turning automation into unsafe trading, spam or platform bypass.

## Brand Personality

Calm, tactical, precise. The interface should feel like a premium operational cockpit: focused under pressure, visually memorable, but never decorative at the expense of control.

## Anti-references

Do not make a generic admin dashboard, marketing landing page, global sidebar dashboard, purple SaaS template or dark-blue observability clone. Do not hide high-risk actions behind pretty UI. Do not merge the three agent workspaces into one system panel.

## Design Principles

1. Workspace first: Trading, Facebook Leads and Platform Content each has its own visual world, menu and operational rhythm.
2. Gated automation: risky actions must show mode, guard state, consequence and audit path before execution.
3. Preserve context: links, notifications, approvals and logs always return to the relevant workspace and record.
4. Fast but accountable: lead and content workflows should reduce delay while keeping draft, consent, proof and platform policy visible.
5. Distinct references, shared discipline: each workspace can borrow strongly from its reference image while sharing component behavior and safety semantics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, reduced-motion safe transitions, keyboard-reachable controls and no color-only risk indicators. Mobile layouts must preserve the workspace toggle and current workspace menu without text overflow.
